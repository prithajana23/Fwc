\begin{align}
    OA &=OB\\
    \theta_2- \theta_1&= 180\degree\\
    \theta_2 &= 307\degree
    \end{align}
Angle between these two tangents is
\begin{align}
    \cos{\theta}&=\frac{\vec{m_1}^{\top}\vec{m_2}}{\vec{\norm{m_1}\norm{m_2}}}\\
