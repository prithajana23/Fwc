    1\\\frac{-8-5\sqrt{3}}{11}\end{pmatrix}$

The normal vector is,$\vec{n_2}=\begin{pmatrix}
    \frac{-8+5\sqrt{3}}{11}\\-1
\end{pmatrix}$or,$\begin{pmatrix}
