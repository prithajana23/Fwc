For getting $\theta_1$,
\begin{align}
    AB&=5\\
    2r\cos{\theta_1}&=5\\
    CD&=11\\
    2r\cos{\theta_2}&=11\\
    or,\frac{\cos{\theta_1}}{\cos{\theta_2}}&=\frac{5}{11}\\
    or,\theta_1&=117\degree
    \end{align}
Two chords are parallel and opposite to each other.So,
\begin{align}
   OA^2&=OC^2\\
   OQ^2+CQ^2&=OP^2+PA^2\\
   \brak{OP-OQ}6&=\brak{\frac{11}{2}}^2\brak{\frac{5}{2}}^2\\
   or,OP-OQ&=4\\
   or,OP=5\\
   So,r=OA=OC=5.59cm
    \end{align}
